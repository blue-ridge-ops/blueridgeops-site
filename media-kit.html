About Blue Ridge Ops

Blue Ridge Ops is a technology consulting firm based in Hamptonville, North Carolina. We deliver hands-on consulting in mobile app development, data design, AI integration, system monitoring, incident management, and production operations. Our approach is straightforward: small team, big results, no fluff.

Founded by Peter, Blue Ridge Ops works with businesses that need real engineering help — not slide decks. We build, deploy, and maintain the systems our clients depend on.

  • Do not place the full-color logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
