Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with companies of all sizes — from startups that need their first production environment to established businesses modernizing legacy systems. If you have a technology challenge, we can probably help.

Do you work with clients outside North Carolina?

Absolutely. While we're based in Yadkinville, NC, most of our technology consulting work is done remotely. We've partnered with companies across the Southeast and beyond.

How quickly can you start on a project?

For most engagements, we can begin within one to two weeks of signing off on the scope. For urgent production support needs, we can often mobilize within 24-48 hours.

What does a typical engagement cost?

It depends on the scope. We offer both project-based pricing and monthly retainers. Every engagement starts with a free discovery call where we'll give you a transparent estimate — no surprises.

Can you work alongside our existing IT team?

That's one of our specialties. We embed directly into your workflow — same tools, same standups, same Slack channels. Your team gets extra firepower without the friction of a traditional vendor relationship.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We're technology-agnostic and choose the right tool for the job. That said, we have deep experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP), modern web and mobile frameworks, SQL and NoSQL databases, CI/CD pipelines, and monitoring stacks.
